Explore Our Rivet Product Range

We manufacture and supply a comprehensive range of rivets to suit all fastening applications. Our rivets are available in multiple materials, head styles, sizes, and finishes to meet the specific demands of each industry.

Blind Rivets

High-performance blind rivets for situations where only one side of the joint is accessible. Perfect for automotive, HVAC, and electrical enclosures, offering strong, vibration-resistant couplings.

POP Rivets

Known for their strong gripping strength and ease of installation, our POP rivets (or pull rivets) are a top choice for light assembly and sheet metal applications.

Aluminium Blind Rivets

Resistant to corrosion with a great strength-to-weight ratio. Widely used in HVAC, signs, and construction, they are ideal for lightweight assemblies.

Aluminium POP Rivets

Combining lightweight design with non-corrosive qualities. Frequently utilized in fields where both functionality and appearance are important.

Aluminium Solid Rivets

The preferred option where strength is crucial. Frequently used in high-load and structural applications, providing a strong and long-lasting fastening in demanding projects.

Custom Rivets

Need something specific? We offer custom rivet manufacturing to meet your exact specifications, including novel head types, coatings, or unusual dimensions.
